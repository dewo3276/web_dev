Early rubber ducks were made from vulcanized rubber. This rubber was so dense, the first ducks never even floated on the water until 1940.

NASA used rubber ducks to trace interior glacial meltflow after a probe that was lowered into the glacier went silent. These 90 ruber ducks were to float out to fisherman who would contact NASA if they found these ducks in certain location across the globe!

Charles Goodyear was the first to invent the rubber duck in the 1800's with the invention of the rubber wheel. These ducks were meant to be chew toys instead of floating bath-time accessories

After a carpenter repainted Buckingham Palace's bathroom, he noticed the queen had a rubber duck with an inflatable crown. Duck sales rose by 80% for a short time after this was reported
